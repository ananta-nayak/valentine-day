Store your media here so the site can play songs and video without opening YouTube.

AUDIO (songs)
  Folder: public/audio/
  Naming: use the YouTube video ID + .mp3
  Examples:
    6FOYnHjTPm0.mp3  → Pehla Nasha (Rose Day)
    3nRzXFgF_1s.mp3  → Tum Hi Ho (Propose Day / Yes Forever)
    w6k3hF2Ox-w.mp3  → Agar Tum Saath Ho (Chocolate Day)
    ... etc for each song in the Valentine Week section.

VIDEO
  Folder: public/video/
  File name: valentine.mp4
  This plays when the user clicks "Watch our video" on the hero section.

You can obtain audio/video through legal means (e.g. purchase, royalty-free, or your own recordings). For personal use, tools like yt-dlp can download from YouTube; ensure you comply with YouTube's Terms of Service and copyright.
